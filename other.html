Music

I play the piano and the guitar and I sing. Back in the days I was the guitarist/lead singer in a rock band and sometimes we even played for more than twenty people. Together with my friend Johannes Lohmann we wrote a musical and a couple hundred people came to see that one. Ask me about this, it's a fun story!
